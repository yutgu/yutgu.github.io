About

I was born and raised in Shanghai, China, and my whole family has been living there since at least the generation of my great grandparents. I am a proud and fluent speaker of Shanghainese [zɑ̃̀hɛ́ ɦɛ̀ɦó] 上海闲话 — definitely not because my dad speaks horrible Mandarin. It's also where I met my husband — we went to the same college.

I am an old soul living in a young (well, at least for now) body. I enjoy comedies from the last century: Seinfeld, Frasier, etc. I listen to classical music, play the flute, and go to symphonies whenever I can.

I traveled across Europe while I was an exchange student at the University of Stuttgart (Universität Stuttgart) in Germany. I have fond memories of Lisbon, Portugal.

Before coming to Irvine, I lived in Philadelphia for over two and a half years, where I made some friends for life.

I am (super) good at tongue twisters.
